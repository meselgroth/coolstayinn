Welcome

Built in 2014, Cool Stay Inn is a distinct addition to Boracay Island and a smart choice for travelers. The excitement of the city center is only 0.4 km away. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations. Offering hotel guests superior services and a broad range of amenities, Cool Stay Inn is committed to ensuring that your stay is as comfortable as possible. Guests of the hotel can enjoy on-site features like free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour front desk, express check-in/check-out, luggage storage, Wi-Fi in public areas. Guests can choose from 9 rooms, all of which exude an atmosphere of total peace and harmony. Besides, the hotel's host of recreational offerings ensures you have plenty to do during your stay. Cool Stay Inn is a smart choice for travelers to Boracay Island, offering a relaxed and hassle-free stay every time.
